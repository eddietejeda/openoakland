Open Oakland

What is OpenOakland?

OpenOakland is a new technology innovation organization created by local technologists, open government advocates and data geeks with an interest in supporting, and educating government staff and implementing tools to help open, improve and engage our local governments.

Why OpenOakland?

In a city with huge potential yet no formal structure to support civic innovation, engagement with the technology community and capacity to implement opensource tools we are building OpenOakland to serve as this backbone.

Our Goals

  • To support the local civic hacking community
  • To demonstrate and encourage civic innovation in Oakland and the East Bay
  • To implement targeted opensource tools for use in Oakland
  • To develop key applications and seed development at hackathons
  • To educate local communities about civic hacking, new technology and opensource tools
  • To educate and demonstrate the power of strategic data use
  • To educate leaders and encourage policies and decisions that promote open government
